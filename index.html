Design is the work itself.

Lead product designer building enterprise B2B SaaS. I move design upstream into discovery, design the systems that scale across teams, and migrate legacy platforms without breaking the customers who depend on them.

I run my own research with enterprise customers and govern design systems across parallel teams, treating the loop with users as the core of the work.

More recently I've designed AI product experiences — a persistent in-product agent and the surfaces that show model outputs to enterprise users. Working directly with ML engineers, I translate what the model can and can't do into product decisions, treating uncertainty, explainability, and trust as design constraints.
